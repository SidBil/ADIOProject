# Adio Collection — Creature Descriptions

**25 creatures across 5 sets, 5 per set.**
Creatures are static sticker-style illustrations — collectible objects, not characters.
Rarity climbs real → whimsical within each set.

---

## Forest Set

| Slot | Name | Rarity | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Squirrel** | Common | A round, chubby squirrel with a big fluffy tail, holding an acorn with both paws. Warm brown and cream. |
| 2 | **Fox** | Common | A sitting red fox with a white-tipped tail curled around its feet. Bright amber eyes, simple and clean. |
| 3 | **Deer** | Rare | A gentle spotted fawn standing still, ears perked forward. Soft tan and cream with delicate eyelashes. |
| 4 | **Owl** | Rare | A round little owl perched on a branch stub, eyes wide, feathers patterned in warm brown and gold. |
| 5 | **Mushroom Deer** | Special | A deer with tiny colorful mushrooms growing along its antlers — red, yellow, and white caps dotted with spots. Otherwise looks just like a normal sweet deer. |

## Ocean Set

| Slot | Name | Rarity | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Crab** | Common | A cheerful round crab with big round eyes, claws held up. Bright coral-orange shell, simple and bold. |
| 2 | **Sea Turtle** | Common | A slow-looking sea turtle with a patterned shell in green and brown, little flippers out to the sides. |
| 3 | **Dolphin** | Rare | A leaping dolphin mid-jump, smooth grey-blue with a pale belly and a big smile. |
| 4 | **Octopus** | Rare | A squishy round octopus with eight curling arms, soft purple-pink with big curious eyes and little sucker dots. |
| 5 | **Glowing Jellyfish** | Special | A bell-shaped jellyfish with long trailing tentacles. Mostly translucent white-blue, with a soft warm glow emanating from its center — like it swallowed a little light. |

## Sky Set

| Slot | Name | Rarity | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Robin** | Common | A small plump robin with a bright orange-red breast, perched with feet gripping an invisible branch. |
| 2 | **Parrot** | Common | A stocky parrot in bright green with a red patch on its head and a yellow beak. Wings folded, looking straight ahead. |
| 3 | **Toucan** | Rare | A toucan with an oversized orange-and-yellow beak, black body, white chest. Bold and graphic. |
| 4 | **Eagle** | Rare | A proud bald eagle, white head and tail, dark brown body, wings slightly spread. Dignified and clean. |
| 5 | **Cloud Bird** | Special | A round, puffy bird made entirely of soft white cloud fluff with stubby little wings. Has a normal bird face and feet, but its body is unmistakably a small cloud. |

## Desert Set

| Slot | Name | Rarity | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Lizard** | Common | A small spiky lizard in sandy orange-brown, caught mid-scurry with one foot raised. |
| 2 | **Roadrunner** | Common | A roadrunner in mid-stride, striped brown and cream, long tail sticking out behind, beak forward. |
| 3 | **Camel** | Rare | A one-humped camel sitting down with legs folded underneath, sandy tan with a calm expression. |
| 4 | **Fennec Fox** | Rare | A tiny fennec fox with enormous ears relative to its small body, big dark eyes, cream-colored fur. |
| 5 | **Cactus Buddy** | Special | A short round cactus — but with a tiny smiling face, two little stick arms, and small stubby legs. Bright green with a pink flower on top. Looks very pleased with itself. |

## Night Set

| Slot | Name | Rarity | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Firefly** | Common | A small beetle with a softly glowing yellow-green tail, wings half-open, simple and small. |
| 2 | **Hedgehog** | Common | A round little hedgehog curled slightly, brown spines on top, cream face and belly, tiny eyes. |
